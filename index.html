Biography

My name is Ola, a young lady with an educational background in marketing.
Originally from Nigeria, I now reside in France, where I am embracing a new culture and lifestyle.
I have a passion for cooking, swimming, cycling, and taking short walks, which help me stay active and engaged with my surroundings.
While my French speaking level is intermediate, I am fluent in English and Yoruba, allowing me to connect with a diverse range of people.
I am particularly drawn to the colors yellow, blue, orange, white, and black, which reflect my vibrant and dynamic personality.
Currently, I am expanding my skill set by studying Cloud Engineering at ALT School, where I am eager to merge my marketing expertise with cutting-edge technology.

Why I decided to study Cloud Engineering in ALTSchool

I decided to study Cloud Engineering because of numerous benefits listed below.
  1. It equips me with the skills to design, deploy, and manage cloud-based solutions, which are in high demand across various industries.
  2. To find a job easily in the Technical domain.
  3. To earn quite well and improve my standard of living.
  4. Ultimately, this field combines technical expertise with innovative problem-solving, making it an exciting and valuable area of study.

My Career Goals

My career goal is to become a proficient cloud engineer, specializing in designing, deploying, and managing scalable cloud-based solutions.
